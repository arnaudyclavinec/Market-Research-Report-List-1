<p><p>The Capsule Endoscopy Robot market consists of two main types: Internally Driven Type and Magnetron Type. The Internally Driven Type utilizes an inherent propulsion mechanism within the capsule, allowing for controlled navigation through the gastrointestinal tract, enhancing image acquisition. In contrast, the Magnetron Type employs external magnetic fields to maneuver the capsule, providing greater flexibility in maneuverability and targeted imaging of specific areas. Both types aim to improve diagnostic capabilities in gastrointestinal disorders while minimizing patient discomfort.</p></p>
