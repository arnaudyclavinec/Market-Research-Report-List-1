Spot welding joins metal sheets at specific points using heat generated by resistance, commonly used in automotive assembly. Other welding techniques encompass methods like laser and electron beam welding, offering precision and versatility for different applications. Together, these technologies drive advancements in manufacturing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
