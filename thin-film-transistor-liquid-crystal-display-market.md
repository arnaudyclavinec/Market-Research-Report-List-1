TN panels are known for fast response times and low production costs, making them ideal for budget monitors. VA panels offer deeper contrast and better color reproduction, suited for multimedia applications. IPS panels provide superior color accuracy and viewing angles, favored for professional graphics and high-end displays, appealing to a diverse range of consumers.</p></p>
