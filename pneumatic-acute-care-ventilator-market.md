<p><p>The Pneumatic Acute Care Ventilator market comprises two main types: non-invasive and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive ventilators provide respiratory support without intubation, using masks or nasal interfaces to assist patients with conditions like COPD or respiratory distress. In contrast, invasive ventilators require endotracheal tubes or tracheostomy for more critical patients needing mechanical ventilation due to severe respiratory failure. Both types are essential in acute care settings, addressing varying patient needs and ensuring effective respiratory management.</p></p>
