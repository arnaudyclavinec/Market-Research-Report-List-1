<p><p>Active and passive automotive safety systems are critical components designed to enhance vehicle safety. Active safety systems involve technologies that prevent accidents by anticipating potential hazards and intervening accordingly. These include features such as anti-lock braking systems (ABS), electronic stability control (ESC), adaptive cruise control, and collision avoidance systems. In contrast, passive safety systems are implemented to protect occupants during an accident, focusing on minimizing injury.
