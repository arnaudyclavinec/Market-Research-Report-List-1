<p><p>The synthetic concrete fibers market is divided into macro-synthetic and micro-synthetic fibers, each serving specific applications in concrete reinforcement. Macro-synthetic fibers are larger, typically used to enhance structural integrity, reduce cracking, and improve overall durability in concrete applications, such as slabs and pavements. Micro-synthetic fibers, on the other hand, are finer and primarily aimed at controlling plastic shrinkage cracking and enhancing the finish of concrete surfaces. Both types contribute to improved performance and longevity of concrete structures.</p></p>
