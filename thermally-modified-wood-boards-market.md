<p><ul><li>Thermo-S</li><li>Thermo-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermally modified wood boards are categorized into two main types: Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S boards undergo a heat treatment process that improves their durability and stability, making them suitable for indoor applications like furniture and flooring. In contrast, Thermo-D boards are designed for outdoor use, featuring enhanced resistance to moisture and decay, ideal for decking and external structures. Both types benefit from reduced environmental impact, as the modification process utilizes heat instead of chemicals, preserving the natural aesthetics of the wood.</p></p>
