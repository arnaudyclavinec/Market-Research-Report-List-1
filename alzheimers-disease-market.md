<p><p>The Alzheimer's disease market is mainly categorized into three types based on key medications: Donepezil, Memantine, and Rivastigmine. Donepezil is a cholinesterase inhibitor that enhances cognitive function by increasing acetylcholine levels. Memantine, an NMDA receptor antagonist, helps to regulate glutamate activity, thereby improving symptoms. Rivastigmine, another cholinesterase inhibitor, is available in various forms including patches. Together, these medications aim to manage symptoms and improve the quality of life for patients, driving demand within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
